# **Partial Payment**

Your customers may request to do partial payments for their transactions.

## **Creating Partial Payments**

<video width="650" height="366" controls>
  <source src="/_content/_partial/25createpartial.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/2.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>1. After creating your transactions, tap on <b>Payment</b> to proceed to the payment screen.</p>

<br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/1.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br>2. This is your payment screen.
<h5>
<ol type="A" style="float:left; margin-left:1rem">
<li>Tap on the customer to add a customer to the ticket/transaction.</li>
<li>This is where your customer's balance will be displayed.</li>
<li>Tick the box beside the "Allow partial payment".</li>
</ol>
</h5>
</p>

<br><br><br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/3.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>3. Tap on the customer icon to add a customer. 
<h5>
<ol type="A" style="float:left; margin-left:1rem">
<li>Search for a specific customer's name</li>
<li>Add a new customer</li>
<li>Select a customer to assign and click <b>Save</b></li>
</ol>
</h5>
</p>

<br><br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/4.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>4. Your partial payment screen should be complete when you have assigned a customer and ticked the "Allow partial payment" box and have input your customer's partial payment. Click <b>Proceed</b> to finish the partial payment.</p>

<br>

## **Paying Partial Payments**

<video width="650" height="366" controls>
  <source src="/_content/_partial/26payingpartial.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

Collecting your customer's balance comes after making partial paymemnts.

<p><img src="_content/_partial/5.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br><br>1. On the <b>Navigation Menu</b>, select <b>Customers</b>.</p>

<br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/6.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br><br>2. Tap on the customer which balance you would like to collect.</p>

<br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/7.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>3. On the edit customer screen, you can find your customers visit information such as their number of visits, last date of visit, and their remaining balance.</p>

<br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/8.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>4. On the <b>Customer Sales</b> tab, you can find all the receipts of the transactions of your selected customer. Tapping on a specific receipt will send you to a detailed screen of the transaction.</p>

<br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/9.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>5. After checking on your customer's balance, on the <b>Navigation Menu</b>, under <b>Shift Management</b>, select <b>Cash Balancing</b>.</p>

<br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/10.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br>6. The cash balancing screen is where you make adjustments to your cash. This is also where you record your customer's balance payments.
<h5>
<ol type="A" style="float:left; margin-left:1rem">
<li>Input customer's payment amount</li>
<li>Add a comment to label what the cash amount is for.</li>
</ol>
</h5>
</p>

<br><br><br><br>

<p><img src="_content/_partial/11.png" alt="nav" width="480" height="270" style="float:left; margin-right:1rem"><br><br><br>7. Select the paying customer and their balance will automatically show. Select <b>Pay In</b> to record incoming cash. And your customer's balance is now paid!</p>
