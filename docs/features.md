### <b>SHIFT MANAGEMENT</b>

> KaHero POS lets you manage the shifts between your
employees and track their working time and sales
transactions.

<video width="650" height="366" controls>
  <source src="/_features/3ShiftManage/3shift.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

#### How to Use:

1.	On the <b>Navigation Menu</b>, Select <b>Shift Management</b>, then select <b>Open Shift</b> to start shift.

<img src="_features\3ShiftManage\1b.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

2.	Input your initial count of cash before the shift, then select <b>Start Shift</b>.

<img src="_features\3ShiftManage\2b.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

3.	To end your shift, go back to the <b>Shift Management</b> and select <b>Close Shift</b> to close shift.
Input your ending count of cash after the shift.

<img src="_features\3ShiftManage\3b.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

4. <b>X-Reading</b> shows the sales report of each shift. Select <b>Print X Report</b> to print X-Reading
of a certain shift.

<img src="_features\3ShiftManage\4b.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

5. <b>Z-Reading</b> shows the sales report of each day. Select <b>Print Z Report</b> to print Z-Reading
of a certain day. You can choose to view the Z-Reading of a specific date.

<img src="_features\3ShiftManage\5b.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

<br>

### <b>QUICKBOOKS SYNCHRONIZATION</b>

<img src="_features\5QB\5qb.png" alt="1a" width="650" height="366">

> KaHero POS works with Quickbooks Online to synchronize
your sales data every shift. Every time you end a shift, Quickbooks
generates a sales receipt with the list of items, discounts, refunds,
cash ins and cash outs, making it easier for you to keep track of
your accounts.
